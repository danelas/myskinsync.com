produces more from the Listener briefs at scale.

**Rules that keep you compliant + effective:**
- Every link goes to **myskinsync.com** (quiz or a guide) — never a raw Amazon link (Amazon
  forbids affiliate links off-site; the affiliate links + disclosure live on your site).
- Put **“Contains affiliate links”** once in your Pinterest/TikTok/IG bio.
- Pinterest is the fast lane for skincare — aim **3–5 pins/day**. TikTok/Reels **1/day**.
- Pin images: 1000×1500 portrait. Keep the **top third clear** for the text overlay.
